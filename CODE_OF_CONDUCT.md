# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want SourceLens-RAG to be an open, respectful, and safe community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions and reviews.
- Focus on technical issues, not personal attacks.
- Accept feedback and collaborate in good faith.
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or intimidation.
- Personal insults, trolling, or repeated disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this policy.
They may remove comments, close discussions, or ban contributors for behavior that violates this code.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private issue or contact the maintainers via repository contact channels.
